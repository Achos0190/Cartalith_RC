About

Deterministic growth simulation: site → anchors → least-cost primary routes → epoch-looped street growth (densification + exploration) → planar-face blocks → series-platted strip parcels → grammar buildings → derived districts → wall & gates → details. The renderer draws only what the simulation produced. Evidence register: docs/03-mathematical-assumptions.md (M-* refs in the inspector). Drag to pan, wheel to zoom.
