About

Deterministic growth simulation: environment/site → anchors → culture-profiled street growth (medieval: epoch-looped organic densification + exploration over least-cost routes; Roman: a planned cardo/decumanus grid laid in one act) → planar-face blocks → culture-profiled parcels (medieval: series-platted strips; Roman: insula lots) → grammar buildings → derived districts → wall & gates → details. The renderer draws only what the simulation produced. Evidence register: docs/03-mathematical-assumptions.md (M-* refs in the inspector and the Evidence Ledger panel); architecture: docs/07-culture-architecture.md. Drag to pan, wheel to zoom.
